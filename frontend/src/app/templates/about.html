Make the World Greener

This project started at the RIPE NCC Green Tech Hackathon of 2025, the OSSFinder. It is a fork of that project, and under construction.

Every small action creates ripple effects. Join us in:

  • Planting urban forests.
  • Reducing carbon footprints where they are big.
  • Surviving climate change, there where it hits first and the hardest.

Mission

To provide search capabilities to help you find information to make the world a little greener ...

Indexes and capabilities

Currently only one index is available, OSSFinder scraped data (see oss4climate), but working on more: Greener Community, Greener House, and Surviving Climate change (all over the world). And also working on adding search capabilities to find faster what one seeks. See the Roadmap.
