Make the World Greener

This project started at the RIPE NCC Green Tech Hackathon of 2025, the OSSFinder. It is a fork of that project, and under construction. See our Roadmap.

Every small action creates ripple effects. Join us in:

  • Planting urban forests.
  • Reducing carbon footprints where they are big.
  • Surviving climate change, there where it hits first and the hardest.

Our Mission

We provide search capabilities to help you find information to make the world a little greener ...

Technology Stack

  • Starlette for backend
  • Typesense for search
  • PostgreSQL for protective admin data
  • Docker for deployment
  • Nginx for routing
  • Jinja2 for templating

Indexes and capabilities

Currently only one index is available, OSSFinder scraped data (see oss4climate), but we are working on more: Greener Community, Greener House, and Surviving Climate change (all over the world). We are also working on adding search capabilities to find faster what one seeks.
